About

TJS Web Design and Business Consulting provides web design and product strategy services to new and small businesses looking to improve their digital presence. We are especially sensitive to the needs of our clients who may not be as comfortable and savvy with technology. We strive to make the technical aspects of web design accessible to our clients and guide them through our process of website design and development.
