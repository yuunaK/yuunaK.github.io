The Stitchery Way

All Stitchery dresses are hand stitched one at a time. Each dress is a hand produced item. Each dress is unique. Each dress is as special as the hand stitched heirloom garments grandmothers and mothers make for their children.

Dresses are made using innovative construction techniques. The design and pattern cutting of our dresses varies with the fabric. I call this approach “found” method since we find our method in each fabric we take up. Found method does not have the cutting and pinning associated with draping.Found method is not about exclusively cutting on the bias, cutting on the grain or against the grain. In our method, the fabric takes center stage in every aspect of garment production: from design to construction. Different types of fabric dictate different methods of fabric processing, garment design, and garment construction.

Fabric cutting and fabric wastage are kept to a minimum. Typical pattern cutting creates waste. With found pattern cutting techniques, this reduces how much fabric is lost in the process of cutting garment patterns. When you pay for a dress, you will be receiving most of the fabric required to make the dress. This is good for you and for the environment.

Dresses are made out of natural fabrics that launder and wear well. We only select natural fabrics that launder well and wear well. This means no visits to dry cleaners or hunting around for specialist cleaners to maintain your garments. Stitchery dresses are made to be worn and laundered and worn again as many times as you wish without having to worry about special maintenance requirements.

All dresses are made with great attention to detail. We carefully select fabrics that are environmentally friendly, sustainable, whenever possible dyed and processed using non-toxic methods. We try to use peace ( ahimsa ) silks - silks produced without killing the silkworm that produces the cocoon from which the silk fiber is reeled. We try to be mindful of the lifecycle from conception to wasteheap. In addition, in the producing the garments, necklines, armlines, waistlines, pleats - all aspects of each dress are designed and constructed so they are comfortable to wear and look attractive.

Hems in our dresses are generous and all our dresses are either constructed of double-layered fabric or fully lined.
