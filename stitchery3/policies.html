Stitchery Policies

Payment: We accept Visa, Mastercard, Paypal.

Shipping: Dress will be mailed via Fedex 2 day delivery as soon as it as it is completed - usually 2 to 3 weeks. We will notify you in case of unforseen delays.

Returns: We want you to be absolutely satisfied with your purchase. If you feel any doubts about the garment, please return the dress with all tags attached and in new unused unworn condition. Please e-mail us within 2 days of receipt to get a Return Authorization Number. We will not accept returns without prior notification.
